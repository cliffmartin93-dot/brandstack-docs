# Introduction

Brand systems today are often documented through static PDFs, slide decks, and fragmented folders.

While these formats work for small teams, they quickly become difficult to maintain as organizations scale.

Design teams maintain visual systems. Marketing teams manage messaging. Product teams integrate brand into interfaces.

Without structured infrastructure, these systems rarely stay synchronized.

BrandStack introduces a new approach.

Instead of treating brand documentation as static content, BrandStack structures brand knowledge as infrastructure.

This allows organizations to maintain consistency, govern brand evolution, and ensure that brand systems scale across products and markets.
